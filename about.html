Amkam DevBootcamp
Gives You a Life Changing Career

Amkam DevBootcamp takes all its students from knowing nothing about programming or web programming or anything related to independent software developer within 5 months of intensive learning and coding.

Next year, we aiming at expanding to big cities around the country, bringing into the door steps of less preveledged students that cannot afford going to Universites or colleges.

Our curriculum is well structured for all levels of students, beginners, and experienced. All that is required from our intakes is that you can read and write and have a laptop computer.

Please contact us via email for further questions about the Bootcamp and as well as enrollment procedures
@amaduskamara@gmail.com

Amkam DevBootcamp Logo

Amkam DevBootcamp is a software development project initiated by Amadu Kamara back in the days at University. It began when Amkam was giving assistance to first year IT students, teaching them coding and application development with Java Netbeans and Android app development with Android Studio. Since then, students were doing great and grasping the concepts of applications development. Hence, as classes continued during holidays,the class was then named Amkam DevBootcamp after the owner, Amkam.
